Baked Potatoes

Why These Are Good

These baked potatoes are so good. They're easy to make, they come out perfect every time, and they're tasty. Let's not forget that sometimes we don't have time for active cooking These are perfect, just preheat the oven, pop em in and set the timer.

One more thing though: don't forget if you're going to make these to leave yourself plenty of time. You will need a couple of hours between preheating the oven, cooking the potatoes, and letting them cool a bit. I advise not to plan on making these unless you're going to be home early.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Chives (optional>

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 425-450
  2. Put Potatoes In
  3. Let cook 1.5 hours
  4. Take out, let col 10 min
  5. Cut open, add salt pepper & garnish
