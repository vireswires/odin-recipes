Best Banana Bread

How Kalin Makes It

Kalin is really awesome at making banana bread. Here's how he does it. First melt the butter. Preheat the oven while melting the butter. Next mash up 3 large bananas. Pour the melted butter into the bananas, stir, and then add the egg. Then add the baking soda, salt, and sugar. Finally add vanilla, and last the flour. If you want you can also add chopped walnuts here. yum!

Pour it into a baking dish, and put it in the preheated oven, set your timer for 45 min, and make sure to check it at that point! If it's too mushy inside, let it go another few minutes, but remember it will continue to cook very slightly after you take it out of the oven. Better a little soft than too dry!

Ingredients

  • 3 Bananas
  • 2/3 cup of Butter
  • less than 1/4 tsp Salt
  • less than 1/3 cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 1/2 cups Flour
