StrawBananasmoothie

Description

This recipe needs no descriptons. Everyone knows what a smoothie is. But In case you don't, you put the ingredients in a blender with some ice. Then you drink it and get ice cream headache.

Make sure you don't wait too long to drink it or it'll get warm. Then it's kinda gross and at least 2x worse than when it's cold. Some people put ice cubes in if it's taking them too long to drink it, but I say that's a bad idea.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Bananas
  • Ice
  • Peanut Butter (optional)

Steps

  1. Put bananas and strawberries in blender with ice
  2. Blend them up!
  3. Drink your smoothie
